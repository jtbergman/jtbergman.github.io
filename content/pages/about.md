### The basics

I am an iOS engineer based in New York City. I currently work on Mobile AI at Notion. Prior to Notion, I worked at Duolingo where I was a member of the Delight Team, Application Stability and Performance Team, and finally the Math Team. I started my career at MicroStrategy (pre-Bitcoin) where I specialed in RxSwift and RealmDB.

A decent amount of my time outside of work is spent either learning Korean or building tools for learning Korean. A long time ago I studied abroad at Yonsei University in Seoul, and this year I am making an effort to achieve conversational fluency. 

### Personal projects

Currently, I am building a local-only language app for studying Korean. The primary focus is interactive vocabulary acquisition with spaced repetition. The vocabulary covered is mined from some of my favorite resources: the _2,000 Essential Korean Words_ series and _TOPIK II Vocabulary in 50 Days_ all by Darakwon.

I am also adding support for sentence mining from learner-provided resources. I intend to make it work particularly well for the Integrated Korean series, Korean Reading with Culture (also Darakwon), and TTMIK's Iyagi series. Sentence mining will feed directly into the spaced repetition system, so this is a natural extension.

Additionally, I am adding a TOPIK prep section based on official exams. Finally, I plan to incorporate some of Mikel's "language islands" advice for speaking practice shared [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fXDcN_Yme5o) and [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O01AD9NgqIQ).

I hope to write more about this project soon. If you're interested, get in touch!
